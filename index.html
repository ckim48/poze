How does POZE work?

Social change starts on the inside. Changing, what we don’t agree with starts with us. Overcoming inequality means triggering a process from the inside out, and from the outside in. A changed reality widens its circles to pull in ever more individuals. A vicious cycle becomes a virtuous cycle. Sustainable development is not merely about resilience, but growth of the individual.
